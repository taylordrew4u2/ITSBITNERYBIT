## Description (4000 characters max)

```
BitBinder is a professional comedy notebook built for stand-up comedians, sketch writers, and anyone who writes jokes. Capture ideas on the fly, organize your material into folders and set lists, record your performances, and sync everything across your devices with iCloud.

WRITE & ORGANIZE
• Capture joke ideas instantly — just open the app and start typing
• Organize jokes into custom folders by topic, style, or venue
• Browse your library in a visual Grid or compact List view
• Search your entire collection to find any joke fast
• Tag jokes for quick filtering
• Mark your best material as Hits for easy access
• Label jokes for Open Mic to track what's stage-ready

BRAINSTORM
• Dedicated brainstorm space for raw ideas and premises
• Voice capture — speak your idea and it's transcribed automatically
• Color-coded sticky notes to visually sort your thoughts
• Promote any brainstorm idea to a full joke with one tap

SET LISTS
• Build performance-ready set lists from your joke library
• Drag to reorder your lineup
• Swipe through jokes on stage during a performance
• Create sets for specific gigs, open mics, or showcases

RECORD & TRANSCRIBE
• Record your performances and practice sessions
• Automatic speech-to-text transcription
• Review what you actually said vs. what you wrote
• Keep an audio archive of your growth

NOTEBOOK SCANNER
• Photograph your handwritten notebook pages
• Digital backup of all your physical material
• Organized photo grid for quick browsing

BITBUDDY — YOUR COMEDY WRITING PARTNER
• Built-in writing assistant available on every screen
• Knows joke structure, comedy theory, and writing techniques
• Ask it to analyze a joke, suggest punchlines, or brainstorm premises
• Understands setups, callbacks, misdirection, rule of three, and more
• Fully on-device — your material stays private

ROAST MODE
• Dedicated mode for writing targeted comedy
• Create roast targets with photos
• Write and organize burns per target
• Track your roast count with a flame meter
• Dark theme with fire accents — gets you in the zone

iCLOUD SYNC
• Sync jokes, set lists, recordings, brainstorm ideas, and notebooks
• Access your material from iPhone, iPad, or any device
• Automatic encrypted backup — never lose your work
• Works seamlessly in the background

DESIGNED FOR COMEDIANS
• Native iOS design — feels like it belongs on your phone
• Auto-save everywhere — your work is always protected
• Import jokes from text files, notes, or other apps
• Export your material as PDF
• No account required — just open and start writing

Whether you're backstage at a showcase, riding the subway home from an open mic, or lying in bed at 2 AM with a premise that won't let you sleep — BitBinder is where your material lives.
```

## App Review Notes

*Information for the App Store review team.*

```
BitBinder is a comedy writing productivity app. No account or login is required.

Key permissions requested:
- Microphone: Used for recording performances and voice-to-text joke capture
- Speech Recognition: Used for automatic transcription of recordings and voice capture
- Camera: Used for scanning physical notebook pages
- Photo Library: Used for importing and saving notebook scan images

The BitBuddy writing assistant runs entirely on-device using a local rule-based system. No user data is sent to external servers for the chat feature.

iCloud sync is optional and uses the user's private CloudKit container. All data stays within the user's Apple ecosystem.

The app contains a "Roast Mode" feature for writing targeted comedy (roast battles are a mainstream form of stand-up comedy). Content is user-generated and private. The app does not include or generate any objectionable content itself.
```
