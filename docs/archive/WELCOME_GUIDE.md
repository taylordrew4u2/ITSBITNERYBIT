# BitBinder - Welcome Guide

Welcome to **BitBinder** — your professional comedy notebook, designed by comedians for comedians.

## What You Can Do

 **Jot down ideas** instantly (like a real notebook)  
 **Organize jokes** into folders and set lists  
 **Record yourself** performing (audio + auto-transcription)  
 **Scan your notebooks** for digital backup  
 **Sync to iCloud** across all your devices  
 **Switch to Roast Mode** for targeted comedy writing  

## 30-Second Quickstart

1. **Tap Notepad** (top of menu)  Type a joke idea  Auto-saves
2. **Tap Jokes**  See your joke library (Grid or List view)
3. **Create a folder** to organize by topic
4. **Record yourself** in Recordings section
5. **Enable iCloud Sync** in Settings to protect everything

That's it! You're ready.

## Your First 5 Minutes

### 1⃣ Open the Notepad (30 seconds)
- Tap **Notepad** in the menu
- Type a joke you've been thinking about
- Close when done (auto-saves)
- Notice the **ruled lines** (32pt—like a real legal pad)
- Notice the **red margin line** (at 52pt—authentic!)
- Notice the **punch holes** (left edge—real notebook feel)

### 2⃣ Check Your Jokes (30 seconds)
- Tap **Jokes** in the menu
- Tap the **Grid/List toggle** (top-left) to switch views
- Grid = beautiful cards, List = compact
- Use whichever you prefer

### 3⃣ Create Your First Folder (30 seconds)
- In Jokes section, tap "+" next to the folder chips
- Name it something like "Observational"
- You can create as many as you want

### 4⃣ Add a Joke (1 minute)
- Tap "+" button (top-right in Jokes)
- Enter a **Title** (the punchline or topic)
- Enter **Content** (full joke or details)
- Choose a **Folder** (or leave blank)
- Tap **Save**

### 5⃣ Enable iCloud Sync (1 minute)
- Go to **Settings** (bottom of menu)
- Tap **iCloud Sync** under "Cloud" section
- Toggle **"Enable iCloud Sync"** ON
- Wait 5 seconds for initial sync
- **Done!** Everything backs up automatically

## Roast Mode Explained

### What is Roast Mode?

A complete theme change for writing targeted comedy about specific people.

### How to Use It

1. **Enable**: Settings  Mode  Toggle "Roast Mode" ON
2. **Entire app goes dark** with fire/flame accents
3. **Menu changes**: 
   - Jokes becomes "Roasts"
   - Sets becomes "Roast Sets"
   - Recordings becomes "Burn Recordings"
   - Notepad becomes "Fire Notepad"
4. **Add targets**: Tap "+" to add people to roast
5. **Write roasts**: Like jokes, but targeted
6. **See flame meter**: Shows roast count per target

### Important

**Jokes and Roasts are completely separate:**
- Normal Mode only shows regular jokes
- Roast Mode only shows roasts & targets
- Switching modes doesn't delete anything
- They live in separate sections

## iCloud Sync (The Easy Way)

### Why You Want This

- **Automatic Backup**: All your material protected
- **Cross-Device**: Access from iPhone, iPad, Mac
- **Peace of Mind**: Never lose work to a broken phone
- **Simple**: Just toggle it on

### How to Enable

1. **Settings**  **Cloud** section
2. Tap **iCloud Sync**
3. Toggle **"Enable iCloud Sync"** ON
4. Wait a few seconds (initial sync)
5. **Done!** All data syncs automatically

### What Syncs

-  Notepad (thoughts & ideas)
-  Jokes & Roasts
-  Set Lists
-  Recordings (audio + transcriptions)
-  Notebook Photos

## Tips for New Users

###  Do These Things

1. **Enable iCloud Sync** right away
2. **Create folders** (organize by topic, style, venue)
3. **Use Grid View** to browse and get inspired
4. **Record yourself** practicing (transcription helps)
5. **Scan your notebooks** for backup
6. **Create set lists** before gigs (builds confidence)

###  Avoid These Things

1. **Don't** assume data is synced immediately (enable iCloud)
2. **Don't** write a huge joke then close without saving
3. **Don't** delete from one device expecting sync to restore
4. **Don't** fight the keyboard (use "Done" button!)
5. **Don't** ignore Roast Mode if you do roasts (it's perfect for it)

## Workflow Ideas

### Daily Routine

1. **Morning**: Open Notepad, add new ideas (observations from yesterday)
2. **Afternoon**: Organize jokes into folders
3. **Evening**: Practice with Recordings
4. **Before bed**: Review tomorrow's set list

### Open Mic Prep

1. **1 week before**: Create "Open Mic - [Date]" set list
2. **3 days before**: Add your best 10-15 jokes
3. **1 day before**: Record yourself performing (full set)
4. **Review transcription** for timing/pacing
5. **Night before**: Mentally review the set
6. **Day of**: Trust your preparation!

### Roast Mode

1. **Enable Roast Mode**
2. **Add targets** (people you're roasting)
3. **Write roasts** (targeted jokes for each person)
4. **Record practice** roasts
5. **Perform at the event**
6. **Disable Roast Mode** when done
7. **Switch back** to regular jokes

## Key Features Explained

### Grid View
- **What**: Beautiful 2-column card layout
- **When**: Browsing for inspiration
- **Why**: You actually see your jokes (not just titles)
- **Toggle**: Tap icon in toolbar (top-left)

### List View
- **What**: Compact single-column layout
- **When**: Quick searching, fast scrolling
- **Why**: Minimal, scannable format
- **Toggle**: Same icon in toolbar

### Folders
- **What**: Categories for your jokes
- **When**: Use always (keeps things organized)
- **Examples**: "Observational", "Relationships", "Work", "Dark"
- **Benefit**: Filter jokes quickly, find what you need

### Recordings + Transcription
- **What**: Record yourself, get automatic transcription
- **When**: Practice sessions, gigs, open mics
- **Why**: Hear how you sound, review timing
- **Bonus**: Transcription shows exactly what you said

### Set Lists
- **What**: Curated groups of jokes
- **When**: Before every gig
- **Why**: Know your flow, builds confidence
- **Usage**: Read on stage, swipe to next
